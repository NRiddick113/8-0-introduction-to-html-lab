Home Fries

Description

Home fries are fried pototes, that and using cook as a breakfast side.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 8 red/golden potatoes
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 color pepper
  • salt
  • pepper
  • sazon

Directions

  1. Cut potatoes into squares
  2. wash potatoes
  3. In a bowl add pototoes, add the garlic powered,salt, sazon, and black pepper, stir to combine.
  4. Place a 12-inch cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add 2 tablespoons of oil to the skillet and swirl it around to coat the bottom of the pan.
  5. Add the potatoes, toss to coat in oil
  6. cut onions and pepper
  7. Add the bell peppers, and onion.Mix
  8. Cover and cook for 20 minutes, stirring the veggies every few minutes, make sure to scrape the bottom of the skillet so nothing gets stuck.
  9. At the 20 minute mark, check to see if the potatoes are cooked through, if they are not, continue to cook until they are.
  10. Taste and add salt if needed, serve immediately.
